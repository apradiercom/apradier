Social Impact through Research


​

I help changemakers build purpose-driven careers, organizations and ecosystems without losing themselves.

Being an changemaker comes without a job description.

Launching a purpose-driven business is exciting and usually driven by the founder's desire to make real impact in their community. At the same time, it often is a lonely, uncertain and overwhelming experience.
Founded in the principles of human-centered design, I can help you get started and answer many of the early questions:
  • Does your idea have the potential to grow into a financially sustainable business?
  • How do you know that customers want your service or product, and are willing to pay for it?
  • How do you plan for social impact and ensure progress toward your goal of social change, while being profitable?
  • What steps do you take in what order to get your business off the ground?​

Managing and growing a purpose-driven business is a marathon, not a sprint. As an entrepreneur, you are constantly required to upgrade your skill set and grow along with the needs of your company. Some of the questions I help entrepreneurs to answer are these:
  • How do you avoid burning out?
  • What systems and processes do you need to work efficiently, be an effective CEO, and grow your company?
  • How do you make sure you work not only IN, but ON your business?
  • How do you ensure you are producing high-quality work?

What is a purpose-driven entrepreneur?

I consider entrepreneurs who want to create a business that does not only turn a profit but that also creates a positive social and/or environmental impact in their communities socially responsible. The spectrum is wide and runs from pure social enterprise after Muhammad Yunus to Certified B Corps. The entrepreneurs I work with want to do exactly that: use their company to create value in their communities. 
MORE ABOUT ME

You don't have to be a starving martyr to make a difference.

Launching and managing a socially responsible business takes some business savvy. I help entrepreneurs launch, manage and grow businesses that make a difference in their community, and allow the team behind it to make a living. No founder under my wings ever feels like they are figuring things out by themselves. I am by their side every step of the way, helping them make hard decisions, holding them accountable and sometimes simply lending an ear to the frustrations of launching and running a business that creates impact. I dedicate a lot of time to staying on top of international research and best practices and building communities of entrepreneurs who use business as a force for good.

If being the starving martyr is your thing, however, go ahead!
